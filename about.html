About Us

Welcome to Yapahuwa Yoga Villa

Welcome to Yapahuwa Yoga Villa, your premier retreat for tranquility and rejuvenation in the heart of Sri Lanka. Surrounded by the natural beauty of Dambulla, Sigiriya, and Habarana, our sanctuary offers a transformative wellness experience, designed to refresh your body, calm your mind, and awaken your inner energy through ancient yoga and holistic healing practices.

Under the expert guidance of Indika Gunarathna, a globally certified yoga instructor, psychological counselor, and Reiki Master, we offer personalized yoga sessions tailored to your unique needs. Whether you're staying at luxurious accommodations or seeking a private retreat, we bring the profound benefits of yoga directly to you across Sri Lanka's most serene locations.

At Yapahuwa Yoga Villa, we welcome all practitioners, from beginners to advanced yogis. Our expertise includes Hatha Yoga, Kundalini Yoga, Reiki Healing, and meditation retreats, ensuring a holistic experience that leaves you feeling revitalized and spiritually connected.

Discover the perfect blend of wellness, yoga, and cultural immersion during your visit to Sri Lanka. Book your personalized yoga journey today at Yapahuwa Yoga Villa and embark on a path toward balance, vitality, and inner peace.

About Indika Gunarathna

With over a decade of experience, Indika is a trusted name in Sri Lanka's yoga community, offering holistic wellness solutions to global yoga enthusiasts.

Indika Gunarathna holds a wealth of qualifications that empower his unique approach to yoga and mental well-being:

Psychological Counselor: Diploma from STI Campus.
Yoga Trainer Certification: Hatha Yoga Dip. (Sri Lanka, India - 2015).
Yoga Therapy Specialist: Diploma (2012/2015, India).
Reiki Master: Certified Levels 1, 2, and 3 (American Board of Hypnotherapy - ABH).
Founder: Yapahuwa Yoga Villa, focusing on holistic well-being and spiritual growth.
