Yoga Classes

Hatha Yoga

+

Hatha Yoga, one of the most practiced forms, emphasizes physical postures and breath control. In Sri Lanka, this practice is perfect for tourists looking for relaxation and rejuvenation, especially in serene settings such as the peaceful beaches or lush hill retreats. It’s an ideal starting point for those new to yoga, helping to improve flexibility and mental calmness while connecting with the island's natural beauty.

Reiki Yoga

+

Reiki Yoga blends traditional yoga practices with energy healing techniques. In Sri Lanka, Reiki classes are offered in wellness centers and Ayurvedic resorts, where the ancient energy of the land complements the therapy. Tourists can enjoy a deep sense of relaxation and emotional healing, making it a unique and restorative experience while surrounded by Sri Lanka’s spiritual energy.

Kundalini Yoga

+

Kundalini Yoga is about awakening the dormant energy within through dynamic movements and breathwork. Sri Lanka, with its spiritual ambiance and tranquil landscapes, provides the perfect environment for this transformative practice. It is ideal for those seeking to enhance their spiritual awareness and embark on a journey of self-discovery amidst the island's sacred temples and serene retreats.

Kriya Yoga

+

Kriya Yoga combines meditation and breath control to reach higher states of consciousness. In Sri Lanka, tourists can engage in this disciplined practice in spiritual retreats surrounded by the island's natural beauty. It is an ideal practice for those who wish to dive deeper into meditation and experience profound mental clarity while exploring the cultural and spiritual heritage of Sri Lanka.

Acupressure Therapy

+

Acupressure Therapy, based on Traditional Chinese Medicine, uses pressure points to release tension and promote healing. Sri Lanka's Ayurveda resorts often incorporate this therapy alongside yoga practices, offering tourists a holistic wellness experience. The peaceful settings of Sri Lanka enhance the benefits of acupressure, helping to relax the body and mind while immersed in the island’s healing environment.

Breathwork

+

Pranayama, or controlled breathing, is a core practice in yoga that helps manage energy flow and stress. Sri Lanka’s serene landscapes provide the ideal environment to practice breathwork, especially in tranquil locations like beaches or hilltops. Tourists can experience reduced stress, enhanced clarity, and improved lung capacity, all while benefiting from the calming atmosphere of the island.
